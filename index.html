About Us

We are a group of people who's main aim is to help people who are tested positive and quarantine for covid 19 and find it difficult to have food at their place as it has been a crucial condition. We are here to help you, you can use our website and send us the contact details. Our volunteers have been taking necessary hygiene precautions by wearing gloves and face masks and using hand sanitizers during food distribution. We feel that as human beings, it's our responsibility to help the most vulnerable amongst us in their time of need.
